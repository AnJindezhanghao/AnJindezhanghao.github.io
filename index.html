Sorting Hat of Sumeru Akademiya






In the long history, Sumeru experienced havoc, and all kinds of dangerous situations were resolved by Blessed One of Wisdom, Mahakusaladhamma. The Greater Lord Rukkhadevata created the rainforest so that the Sumeru people could live in peace. The original akademiya of Sumeru was composed of scholars who had been following the Greater Lord Rukkhadevata for a long time. Each of them carried out his duties to assist Rukkhadevata in managing Sumeru and in understanding Rukkhadevata’s power and philosophy. When the akademiya was founded, the Greater Lord Rukkhadevata established six darshans to represent the six different aspects of wisdom. Numerous darshans of thought rose and faded among the history, but only six darshans,gained permanent seats in the akademiya after a long time.
